[v1.0] — Initial Release
--------------------------

OVERVIEW
  littleduck is a custom Kodi skin built on Estuary (Kodi's default skin) and redesigned
  from the ground up with a Netflix-inspired dark aesthetic and a duck gold accent theme.
  It is built around TMDb Helper and Rabitewatch for seamless content discovery and playback.

VISUAL THEME
  - Netflix Dark palette: near-black background (#141414), deep panels (#1A1A1A / #242424)
  - Duck Gold accent colour (#FCC200) used on focused sidebar items, clock, and highlights
  - Custom duck logo and sidebar icons throughout the interface
  - Roboto-Thin 70pt clock in the top-bar, rendered in gold

HOME SCREEN — SIDEBAR MENU
  The sidebar features six curated sections visible by default:
    • Home        — Continue Watching + trending content via Rabitewatch
    • Films       — Popular, Top Rated, Now Playing, Upcoming, and Trending movies via TMDb
    • TV Shows    — Popular, Top Rated, On the Air, Airing Today, and Trending series via TMDb
    • Anime       — Trending, Popular, and Anticipated anime series and movies via Trakt/TMDb
    • Lists       — Trakt Watchlist (movies & TV), Top Rated collections
    • Favourites  — Kodi Favourites browser

  Each section has up to 5 widget rows configurable from Skin Settings.
  Hidden by default (can be re-enabled in Skin Settings): Add-ons, Music, Music Videos,
  Live TV, Radio, Pictures, Videos, Weather, Games.

WIDGETS SYSTEM
  - Widgets are powered by TMDb Helper (plugin.video.themoviedb.helper)
  - Rabitewatch provides the "Continue Watching" widget on the Home section
  - Widget layout types: BigLandscape (first/hero slot), BigPoster (remaining slots)
  - Widgets can be customised, reordered, or replaced via Skin Settings » Manage Widgets

SEARCH
  - Search is routed exclusively through TMDb Helper
  - Removes all dependencies on FEN, FENLight, Umbrella, Seren, and POV
